    BuzzinArts web application is a pinboard style Social Arts News and Community application for arts people.     BuzzinArts Global Network

    Now it is the time to take this to next level by launching a new destination for arts people. BuzzinArts Global- an invitation only Arts destination on Slack focused towards arts people from everywhere.



    Why bother joining BuzzinArts on Slack? (“What is there for me in it?” question answered here)

    To put it simply, When last checked there were 941,193,999 websites on internet and by the time you are done reading this page internet will be grown by 1,612,300 gigabytes which also contains user generated content by more than 1.6 billion social network users worldwide on various social networking sites, 2,866,979 new Google search queries will be performed, 5,399,97 tweets on Twiiter and 102,298 Skype calls will be made at the rate of 1,746 calls per second and guess what? these numbers are getting bigger and bigger by every second while you are wondering why on earth should I care about this at all?

    Clearly it's a huge information overflow. No one can make sense of such huge amount of information unless it is put in right context. We at Art Frontier Project deeply believe in approaching such challenging questions with profound experimentation on all levels. It seems #curatedcontent by experts can solve problem of contexual as well as relevant information for you hence you can only focus on information that matters to you most. Come on in to join experts and seasoned professionals on BuzzinArts Global

    And how we'll get there sooner via BuzzinArts Global?

    You must be familiar with popular team communication platform - Slack. With Direct messages, channels, private groups and full text search (thanks to great technology by Slack), possibilities are limitless.
    You can observe stream of arts news flooding your notification centre or connect with old or new arty friends, share ideas, notes, documents and artworks with your network or potential sponsors, plan projects or collaborate on exhibitions or reserach projects with people you already know or with art world friends from anywhere around the globe or you can figure out if something else can be done becuase things change when you are directly in touch with global arts professionals’ pool.


    Join BuzzzinArts Global by filling a simple form and explore the arts world the way never done before.
